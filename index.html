My Erasmus projects:

During my Erasmus project in Portugal, I had the opportunity to intern at a dynamic company where I gained valuable hands-on experience in IT. My main responsibilities included installing and configuring Windows 10 operating systems, troubleshooting software and hardware issues, and providing technical support to end-users. This practical experience not only enhanced my technical skills but also improved my problem-solving abilities and teamwork. This experience not only broadened my cultural horizons but also fostered lasting friendships and a deeper sense of global community.
